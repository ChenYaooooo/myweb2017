Reverse Integer

需要考虑的问题：
  • 1. If the integer's last digit is 0, what should the output be? ie, cases such as 10, 100.
  • 2. Did you notice that the reversed integer might overflow? Assume the input is a 32-bit integer, then the reverse of 1000000003 overflows. How should you handle such cases? 32位：-2147483648~2147483648 这个还是挺烦的，因为反过来不能超过它；自己看着办吧
  • 3. 负数：-123情况也没有考虑到
